Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how DirectLandlord collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • User preferences

Property Information

  • Property details and photos
  • Location information
  • Rental prices and terms
  • Contact information

Usage Information

  • Search queries and preferences
  • Saved properties
  • Interaction with listings
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Connect landlords and rent seekers
  • Verify user identities and property listings
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate important updates
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We share information in these situations:

  • Between Users: To facilitate connections between landlords and rent seekers
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or sale

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data transmission (SSL encryption)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage

While we strive to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update

Access and update your personal information in your account settings

Delete Account

Request deletion of your account and personal data

Opt-Out

Opt-out of marketing communications at any time

Data Export

Request a copy of your personal data

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze how our platform is used
  • Improve performance and security
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the platform or email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information:

Email: privacy@directlandlord.com
