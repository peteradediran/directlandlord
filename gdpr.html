GDPR Compliance Statement

General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679
GDPR Compliant

Last Updated: December 2024

DirectLandlord is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights in accordance with the GDPR.

1. GDPR Overview

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union regulation that strengthens data protection for individuals within the EU. Even though we are based in Nigeria, we extend GDPR-level protections to all our users worldwide.

2. Our Data Protection Principles

We adhere to the following GDPR principles:

Lawfulness & Transparency

We process your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently. We clearly explain how we use your data.

Purpose Limitation

We only collect data for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes.

Data Minimization

We only collect data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.

Accuracy

We keep your data accurate and up-to-date. You can update your information anytime.

Storage Limitation

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected.

Integrity & Confidentiality

We process your data securely to protect against unauthorized access or loss.

3. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of your personal data we hold.

Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Restriction

You can request restriction of processing your data.

Right to Data Portability

You can receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Right to Object

You can object to processing of your personal data.

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

Right to Lodge Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  1. Contractual Necessity: To provide our services and facilitate connections between landlords and rent seekers.
  2. Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  3. Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, ensure security, and prevent fraud.
  4. Consent: For specific purposes where you have given explicit consent.

5. Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we do this, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for countries with approved data protection laws
  • Other lawful transfer mechanisms as permitted by GDPR

6. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

As required by GDPR Article 37, we have appointed a Data Protection Officer:

  • DPO Email: dpo@directlandlord.com
  • Responsibilities: Monitoring GDPR compliance, advising on data protection, and serving as a point of contact for data subjects and supervisory authorities.

7. Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure data security:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Property Listings: Retained for 1 year after expiration
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 3 years for quality improvement

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies in compliance with GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive. We:

  • Obtain explicit consent for non-essential cookies
  • Provide clear information about cookie usage
  • Offer cookie preference management tools
  • Document all cookies used on our platform

10. Data Protection Impact Assessments

We conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs) for high-risk processing activities, including:

  • Large-scale processing of sensitive data
  • Systematic monitoring of public areas
  • Processing involving new technologies

Exercise Your GDPR Rights

To exercise any of your GDPR rights or if you have questions about our GDPR compliance:

Data Protection Officer: dpo@directlandlord.com

General Inquiries: privacy@directlandlord.com

Postal Address: Data Protection Office, DirectLandlord, Lagos, Nigeria

Response Time: We aim to respond to all GDPR requests within 30 days.

11. Updates to This Statement

We may update this GDPR Compliance Statement to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes.

12. Supervisory Authority

If you believe we have not addressed your concerns adequately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. For EU residents, this is typically the data protection authority in your country of residence.
